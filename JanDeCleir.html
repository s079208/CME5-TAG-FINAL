Jan de Cleir

Jan Amanda Gustaaf Decleir is a Belgian actor. Decleir is a stage actor, but as a film actor he has reached a larger audience and world fame. Decleir studied for several years at the Herman Teirlinckn Studio, where he also took the lead a few years later with Ivo Pauwels. He renewed the renowned drama school and brought in many new teachers: actors from the Blauwe Maandag Compagnie, STAN and others. He could not prevent the school from being hampered by ever new austerity measures.

He also appeared more often on television in various programmes, for example, you could see him next to Gaston and Leo in the comedy toppers with the play "the tiger".
