Charissa Parassiadis

Charissa Parassiadis or better known as "Slongs" is now a solo artist but used to play with "halven euro". She did have a duel with Adil El Arbi to see who is the best, Antwerp or Brussels. Slongs has a Greek grandfather, a sailor who met his wife in Sinaai. You may also have seen her in the kitchen, that is to say in sofie's kitchen, the cooking programme on vtm. In Alex Agnew's podcast "Welcome to the AA", she came to talk about the old days and how everything was better.
