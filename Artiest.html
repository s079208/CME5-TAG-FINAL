Bart Albert Liliane De Wever is a Belgian Flemish-nationalist politician. He has been party chairman of the New Flemish Alliance (N-VA) since 2004. Since 1 January 2013, Bart De Wever has been the mayor of Antwerp. He has been on various television programmes, such as: De Slimste mens, Winteruur and Gert Late Night.
