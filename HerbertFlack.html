Herbert Flack

Herbert Flack spent his youth in Boom and went on to study at the Royal Flemish Music Conservatory in Antwerp, theatre department. In 1971 he graduated magna cum laude.

He was married for 43 years to Mimi Peetermans (died in early 2014), one of his former teachers. In September 2014, he married again. His wife, Fabienne Arras, died in Spain on 2 July 2020. At the funeral, the song "als ik zou sterven" (if I were to die) by Wannes van de Velde was played. He also attended the funeral of Gaston Begmans, a good friend of his, in 2016.

He is also a man of the kitchen, and likes to cook. That is why he has participated in the "kitchen of sofie".
