Alex Agnew

Alexander John Anthony "Alex Agnew" is a Flemish cabaret artist, stand-up comedian, singer, podcast host and occasional radio presenter. Agnew became known when he was the first Belgian to win the Leids Cabaret Festival in February 2003. He was the frontman of the metal/hard rock band Diablo Blvd. This existed from 2005 until December 2018. Alex Agnew, in his smallest form, can be called an artist.

Since April 2018, Agnew, together with Diablo Blvd guitarist Andries Beckers, has been releasing a weekly podcast titled "Welcome to the AA".
