Erik Van Looy

Erik Van Looy is a Belgian film director, also known as television personality, quiz presenter and voice actor. He directed "The Alzheimer's Case", "Loft", "The Prime Minister" and many more. He also presents "The smartest person in the world", he even participated in Hollywood to co-direct the American version of "Loft". However, this did not turn out to be a good story. He was also a guest in several programmes, such as "Welcome To The AA", "Winteruur" and "Gert Late Night".
